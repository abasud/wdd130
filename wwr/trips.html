Know Our Trips

Colorado River

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime rafting journey through the heart of the Grand Canyon. This 7-day adventure combines adrenaline-pumping rapids like Lava Falls and Crystal with serene stretches of water flanked by towering red rock walls. Along the way, explore hidden waterfalls, ancient petroglyphs, and camp under the vast desert sky. Perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers, this trip promises an unforgettable mix of excitement and tranquility.

Pacuare River

Dive into the lush beauty of Costa Rica’s rainforest with a rafting trip on the Pacuare River, often hailed as one of the world’s most scenic rafting destinations. This 2-3 day trip takes you through a tropical paradise, with cascading waterfalls, vibrant wildlife, and exhilarating Class III-IV rapids. Along the way, enjoy eco-lodges and cultural immersion with local communities.

Sun Kosi River

Known as the "River of Gold," the Sun Kosi offers a world-class rafting experience amidst the breathtaking Himalayas. This 10-day journey covers over 270 km, starting with calm waters and gradually building to powerful Class III-V rapids. Explore traditional Nepali villages, camp on sandy riverbanks, and marvel at panoramic mountain views.
